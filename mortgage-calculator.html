Affordability Guidelines

  • Gross Debt Service (GDS): Should not exceed 32% of gross monthly income
  • Total Debt Service (TDS): Should not exceed 40% of gross monthly income
  • Minimum Down Payment: 5% for homes under $500k, 10% for portion over $500k
  • CMHC Insurance: Required for down payments under 20%

Current Interest Rates

Interest rates vary by lender and term. As of 2024, rates typically range from 5.0% to 7.0%. Contact Mac for current rate information from preferred lenders.

Mortgage Insurance

If your down payment is less than 20%, you'll need mortgage default insurance (CMHC). This protects the lender and is added to your monthly payments.

Additional Costs

Don't forget closing costs (1.5-4% of purchase price), home inspection, legal fees, and moving expenses when budgeting for your home purchase.
