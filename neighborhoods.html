Downtown Edmonton

Urban living at its finest

Average Price: $350,000 - $800,000

About Downtown

Edmonton's vibrant downtown core offers the ultimate urban lifestyle with high-rise condos, converted lofts, and modern apartments. Perfect for professionals who want to walk to work, enjoy nightlife, and experience cultural attractions.

Housing

  • High-rise condominiums
  • Converted loft apartments
  • Modern penthouses
  • Heritage building conversions

Amenities

  • Rogers Place (NHL arena)
  • Winspear Centre (symphony)
  • Art Gallery of Alberta
  • Downtown farmers market

Schools

  • MacEwan University
  • NAIT Downtown Campus
  • Several private schools
  • Near UofA campus

Market Trends

  • Growing condo market
  • Increasing property values
  • High rental demand
  • New developments planned

Old Strathcona

Historic charm meets modern living

Average Price: $300,000 - $650,000

About Old Strathcona

Edmonton's cultural heart, featuring heritage buildings, eclectic shops, and vibrant street life. This historic district combines character homes with modern condos, perfect for those who appreciate arts, culture, and community.

Housing

  • Heritage character homes
  • Modern infill houses
  • Boutique condominiums
  • Artist lofts

Amenities

  • Whyte Avenue shopping
  • Old Strathcona Farmers Market
  • Numerous restaurants & pubs
  • Princess Theatre

Schools

  • University of Alberta nearby
  • Strathcona High School
  • Several elementary schools
  • King Edward School

Market Trends

  • Strong heritage preservation
  • Popular with young professionals
  • Consistent property appreciation
  • High walkability score

Westmount

Prestigious family living

Average Price: $400,000 - $750,000

About Westmount

One of Edmonton's most desirable neighborhoods, Westmount offers tree-lined streets, excellent schools, and upscale shopping. This mature community features beautiful heritage homes and modern infills, perfect for families seeking quality and prestige.

Housing

  • Grand heritage homes
  • Luxury infill properties
  • Executive condominiums
  • Character bungalows

Amenities

  • 124th Street shopping
  • Westmount Shopping Centre
  • Fine dining restaurants
  • Beautiful parks & playgrounds

Schools

  • Westmount Elementary
  • Victoria School of Arts
  • Strathcona High School
  • Several private schools

Market Trends

  • Premium neighborhood pricing
  • Strong resale values
  • Limited inventory
  • High demand from families

Glenora

Elegant riverside living

Average Price: $450,000 - $900,000

About Glenora

Perched on the North Saskatchewan River valley, Glenora is Edmonton's most exclusive neighborhood. Known for its stunning architecture, mature trees, and proximity to downtown, it offers luxury living with natural beauty.

Housing

  • Luxury estate homes
  • Historic mansions
  • Modern architectural gems
  • River valley properties

Amenities

  • Government House Park
  • River valley trail system
  • Close to downtown
  • Exclusive golf clubs nearby

Schools

  • Glenora Elementary
  • Victoria School of Arts
  • Private school options
  • Easy access to top schools

Market Trends

  • Ultra-premium market
  • Exceptional appreciation
  • Very limited supply
  • International buyer interest

Riverbend

Modern family community

Average Price: $300,000 - $550,000

About Riverbend

A thriving family community in south Edmonton, Riverbend offers excellent schools, parks, and shopping. This newer neighborhood combines suburban comfort with urban amenities, making it ideal for growing families.

Housing

  • Modern single-family homes
  • Townhouse complexes
  • New construction
  • Variety of lot sizes

Amenities

  • Riverbend Square shopping
  • Terwillegar Recreation Centre
  • Adventure Park
  • Multiple playgrounds

Schools

  • Riverbend Elementary
  • Lilian Osborne High School
  • Ste. Marguerite Bourgeoys
  • New school developments

Market Trends

  • Popular with families
  • Strong resale market
  • Continued development
  • Good value for money

Summerside

Contemporary lakeside living

Average Price: $350,000 - $600,000

About Summerside

Edmonton's premier lake community featuring a man-made lake, beach, and water activities. This master-planned community offers resort-style living with modern homes, excellent amenities, and a strong sense of community.

Housing

  • Lakefront properties
  • Modern family homes
  • Luxury townhomes
  • Custom-built houses

Amenities

  • Summerside Beach & Lake
  • Community Centre
  • Walking trails
  • Shopping plaza

Schools

  • Summerside Elementary
  • Archbishop Jordan High
  • Nearby Catholic schools
  • Planned new schools

Market Trends

  • Premium community pricing
  • Strong property values
  • High demand location
  • Ongoing development
